Tutoring from EZLOGIC

Free tutoring can be great, but the downside to "free" is that the volunteer tutors may not get around to answering your questions, or they may not answer quickly. Paid tutoring at least guarantees some sort of reply. EZLOGIC's creator, Wayne Dayata, offers e-mail or video conference tutoring at the rate of P100/hour (for email/written) or P150/hour (for video/oral with whiteboard), both rates inclusive of the personalized instructional materials.

If you are interested in availing of the service, kindly fill in the form above or send an email to eztutorials@gmail.com with the subject "Tutorial Request", indicating your name, year level, and fields/topics of interest, and the mode of tutoring (email/video). A PayPal link will then be sent to you via the email address you provided or sent with.

Once your payment has been received, a confirmation email will then be sent to you. Then you can send your questions within the given alloted time and wait for the supporting materials or conference link. Kindly include the area and topic of study (as well as any additional notable instructions) and make your scope as clear as possible so that your concerns can be accurately addressed.

Please do note that as a rule, I do not do homeworks or directly provide the answers of the questions given, nor to take any test or exams in your behalf. I am only limited to explaining concepts, showing how to set up a problem, giving hints, and trying to get you started toward your success. If your text does not include the answers, I may give you these answers so you can check your work. But do not expect a reply that can be copied into longhand and then handed in to your teacher as your own work. Tutoring replies are supplied "as is", with no grade guarantees.
