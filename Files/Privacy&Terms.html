The only way we would receive any of these personally-identifying information would be for the visitor to contact us and provide this information to us directly, such as by sending a message via the site's contact form. As mentioned, none of this information is ever shared with anyone outside of the EZ Tutorials Team nor with any third-parties.

If our information practices change at some time in the future, we will post the policy changes to our Web site to notify you of these changes and we will use for these new purposes only data collected from the time of the policy change forward. If you are concerned about how your information is used, please check back at our Web site periodically.

Users may prevent their information from being used for purposes other than those for which it was originally collected by e-mailing us at the above address or using our contact form. Furthermore, should they to have their information updated or corrected, they may send us an email through the address provided.

If you feel that this site is not following its stated information policy, you may contact us at the our e-mail address, or through our contact form.
